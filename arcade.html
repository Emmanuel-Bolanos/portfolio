OBJECTIVE

This project was made by me and a colleague during my second year of college, the objective was to take advantage of a PIC 18F4550 microcontroller and make an arcade game with the features of our choice.


Our final circuit was inside a plywood structure that we made by hand and the targets were designed and printed by us laser printer. The game also included a laser gun that was made by taking apart a plastic toy gun and assembling it with the components inside. The game was powered using a DC voltage source that we previously build.


The game accepted two players that would compete for 60 seconds and the one who got to shoot most of his objectives would be the winner

TECHNICAL DETAILS

    This project was made using:

  • PIC microcontroller
  • MPLAB IDE
  • C
  • LCD Display
  • Photoresistors
  • DC Voltage Source
  • Laser Printing
  • Woodworking
