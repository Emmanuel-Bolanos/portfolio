ABOUT ME

I live in the beautiful Yucatán Peninsula, I work in the Ksquare University as a JavaScript Associate Software Engineer, my main task at the moment is to complete my training and obtain a certification to demonstrate my skills.

I have a bachelors degree in mechatronics engineering and thanks to my dedication I obtained the award for performance excellence in México for the 2020 period.

During my career, working with microcontrollers and the IoT ignited my interest for computer vision and this inspired me to go even beyond and learn to implement machine learning models for object recognition and tracking in my final year.

My mechatronics background taught me to adapt to new scenarios, to learn fast and to be a problem solver. When I began to take part in more ambitious programming projects, I realised that my past experiences still had value in this field. Nowadays, when I work in a project I use it as an opportunity to learn something new and add more value to the experience.
